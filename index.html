A battery backup system is a device that stores electricity generated by solar panels, wind turbines, or other renewable energy sources to provide power when there is an outage. This type of system can be installed on the home level or for larger buildings like schools, hospitals, and offices. A battery backup system will work seamlessly with solar panel systems because it charges the batteries during the day when the sun’s rays are strongest. The ability to store excess electricity generated from renewable sources throughout peak hours means that less reliance needs to be placed on traditional power plants which use fossil fuels as their primary source of energy generation.

A battery backup system has many benefits including being able to reduce your dependence on fossil fuels and increase your independence from utility companies who may not always have the proper resources available to provide you with electricity. In addition, depending on how much power the battery backup system can store and how many energy sources it can draw from will determine whether or not your energy needs are met in an outage situation.
